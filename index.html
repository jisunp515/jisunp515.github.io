About Me

Hello! I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Operations Research & Financial Engineering (ORFE) at Princeton University, working with Prof. Bartolomeo Stellato.

My research interest lies in mathematical optimization and theoretical aspects of machine learning. My recent work is closely related to the classical fixed-point iterations, understanding them in theoretical and practical perspectives and designing the algorithms upon such understandings.

I obtained my Ph.D. from the Department of Mathematics Sciences at Seoul National University (SNU). My advisor was Prof. Ernest K. Ryu.
